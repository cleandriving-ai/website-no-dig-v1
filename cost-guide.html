Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the lining process take? +

Most residential projects are completed in 1 business day. We typically arrive in the morning, clean the pipe, install the liner, and have you back in full service by the afternoon.

Is the new pipe as strong as PVC? +

Actually, it's often stronger. The structural liner we use is a seamless, epoxy-hardened composite that is practically impervious to root intrusion and has a design life of 50+ years.

Can you line any pipe? +

We can line almost all sewer and storm pipes. However, if a pipe has completely collapsed (flattened), a small spot-repair might be needed before lining. We use CCTV inspections to determine this first.

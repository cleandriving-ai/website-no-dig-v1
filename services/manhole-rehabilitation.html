Manhole Rehabilitation

Structural restoration and inflow/infiltration (I&I) prevention for municipal and industrial sewer systems.

Request Technical Specs Speak to an Engineer

Stop Corrosion.
Restore Integrity.

Aging manholes are the primary entry point for groundwater infiltration into sewer systems. Our multi-stage rehabilitation process restores structural strength and provides a chemical-resistant barrier.

Using advanced cementitious mortars and epoxy coatings, we eliminate the need for costly full replacements while extending the asset's life by up to 50 years.

  • ✓ Fast Return-to-Service Times

Our Rehab Solutions

Epoxy Coatings

High-build, 100% solids epoxy liners that provide superior chemical resistance against hydrogen sulfide (H2S).

Cementitious Liners

Fiber-reinforced mortars used to restore structural integrity to crumbling brick or concrete manhole walls.

Inflow Prevention

Specialized seals and chimney inserts to stop groundwater from entering the system through the manhole frame.
